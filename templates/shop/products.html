Tasting Notes

A true "taste of place" — full-bodied yet approachable, with a flavor that changes with each season's bloom.

🎨

Color

Golden amber to dark amber, depending on the season's nectar sources.

👃

Aroma

Floral, earthy, and slightly woody, reflecting the diverse vegetation of North Florida.

🍯

Flavor

Rich and complex, with layered sweetness, mild floral notes, and subtle hints of herbs, wild berries, and tree blossoms.

🥄

Texture

Thick and smooth when fresh. Because it's raw and unfiltered, it may naturally crystallize over time — a sign of pure honey.
