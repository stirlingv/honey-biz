Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you place an order or submit a request on our website, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Shipping/billing address
  • Order details and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate about your order status
  • Respond to your questions or requests
  • Improve our products and services

Payment Information

We do not store credit card information. All payment processing is handled securely by QuickBooks Payments. Your payment details are entered directly on their secure, PCI-compliant servers and never pass through or are stored on our website.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Payment processors (QuickBooks Payments) to complete transactions
  • Shipping carriers to deliver your order
  • As required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage of customer data
  • Limited access to personal information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Bear Creek Apiaries & Honey LLC
Email: info@bearcreekapiaries.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
